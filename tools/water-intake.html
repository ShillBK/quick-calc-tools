Recommended Daily Intake

Basis: ~30–35 ml per kg body weight (common India guideline) + adjustments for activity, warm Bengaluru climate (~32°C March avg), and exercise (extra ~0.4–0.6 L/hour). Includes all fluids (water, tea, etc.); aim for mostly plain water.

Listen to your body – urine pale yellow = good hydration. Consult doctor if health conditions.
