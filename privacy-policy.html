Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

CalcHub[](https://shillbk.github.io/quick-calc-tools) is a free, personal project providing online calculators. We take your privacy seriously.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. No login, no email, no name, no cookies for tracking.

All calculations happen entirely in your browser (client-side JavaScript). No data is sent to any server.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Tailwind CSS via CDN (no tracking). No analytics, no ads (yet), no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel.

3. Changes to this Policy

If we add features (e.g., contact form, ads) in future, this policy will be updated. Check back occasionally.

Questions? Reach out via the Contact Us page.
