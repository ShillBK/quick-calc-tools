About Us

About CalcHub

CalcHub is a small, free online calculator collection created by Shilpa from Bengaluru, Karnataka.

Started in March 2026 to provide simple, no-ads, no-login tools useful for everyday life in India — especially Bengaluru.

What We Offer

  • Vehicle tools (Fuel Cost, Mileage, Petrol vs EV)
  • Finance calculators (EMI, GST, PPF, Income Tax)
  • Health & fitness (BMI, TDEE/Calorie)
  • Real estate & land (Land Area Converter, Stamp Duty)
  • Education & more (JEE/NEET Rank Estimator, Vastu Guide)

Everything is 100% client-side (runs in your browser). No data stored, no tracking.

Why We Built This

Many free calculator sites are full of ads or require login. CalcHub is clean, fast, and made for real people in Bengaluru/India who need quick calculations without hassle.
